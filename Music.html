Music

Artist Showcase

Music is incredibly important, it brings us together as a community, soothes our minds and allows us to really connect with ourselves and our families! We aim to foster a space where we can all appreciate the music around us, whether it's from old artists or new, local or those who've travelled from afar. We welcome all types of music and invite our visitors to stop and appreciate it with us!

Everyone is welcome here, we bring a celebratory and inclusive atmosphere to bring our community together and really celebrate the community and the people in it. Our goal is to bring together a like-minded community of music lovers who can join together and share their thoughts and their talents in a space tailor made for them!

Owen Whitfield is a semi-local musician who has honed his craft over 7 years. With his riveting piano melodies and intricate compositions that bring a tear to even the most emotionless of people, Owen is truly a master of his craft!

Callum Grey, a young and upcoming talent, wows every stage they step on, with their haunting voice and delicate violin. They bring an eerie beauty to each show and bring not just music, but an atmoshpere with them.

Nabi is a soloist from all the way over in Skipton, bringing her incredible voice to grace our stage. With her covers of songs from all walks of life, you're guarranteed to find something you'll like!

Alex Pallon puts the 'Alt' into Alternative Rock, with his unique style and killer music, he creates an atmosphere to be enjoyed by everyone who stops to listen!
