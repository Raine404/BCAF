What's On

At Burnley Community Arts Festival, we are committed to bringing you an unforgettable experience that you'll never forget. We offer a variety of stalls for everyone! Families and friends are encouraged to visit workshops, see performances and experience the arts!
We often fail to appreciate all the hard work that goes into each and every thing you can find at the festival so feel free to chat to artists and vendors to learn more and meet new people!

Music

We asked our staff what makes a festival. Music was the near unanimous answer, and we know, just as you do, that no festival is truly complete without music! Often, we struggle to broaden our horizons and discover the gems hidden beneath mainstream music, so we do our part to shine a light on your local artists and bring them to your attention! You never know, the next big thing could be your next door neighbour!

Food

We can't survive without food, you can't survive without food, and this festival certainly can't survive without food! Thankfully we have a wonderful selection of stalls and vendors who are more than willing to share their culinary skills and experitse. Truly, the hardest part of coming to BCAF is choosing where you're going for lunch!

Art

Our festival is built around welcoming the arts into Burnley! Welcoming local craftspeople alongside small artists lets our festival really embody the arts alongside bringing you new and wonderful things to find and maybe even try for yourself someday!

Workshops

Festivals are truly only fun when everyone gets involved, whether it's you, your children, your parents, BCAF can promise there'll be something for all! Be prepared to visit the workshops spotted all over the site and get ready to create some art of your own!
