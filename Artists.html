Artists

As an Arts Festival, what is more important than the artists! As a spotlight here we have three incredible local artists all prepared to share their work and skills with the Burnley Community! Our festival aims to incorporate a wide variety of art and talent, especially showcasing the youth in our area!!!
Ensuring that every member of our community is represented and allowed to shine is what makes Burnley Community Arts Festival so special!

Raine

Raine, going by the username raine.5149 on Instagram is a young and talented queer artist, dedicated to sharing their stories and characters online. As it's their first time at such an event they are excited to share and sell copies of their currently released art alongside special art for their up and coming graphic novel! Not only this, they specialise in dreamlike landscapes and scenery (as well as the odd Pokemon here and there!)

Emilia Whalley

Emilia Whalley, while a little outside of Burnley, is an incredibly talented rising artist. Her works often transform real-life photos into elegant paintings, the soft atmoshpheres in each artwork conveying a depth of feeling. No novice to the art world, Emilia embodies creativity and passion when it comes to creating her work. Her works are sure to appeal to anyone and for this year's Festival only! She will be completing requests on site! So sit back, relax and watch a master work at this years Burnley Community Arts Festival!

Evan W

Evan W is a student at our very own Burnley College, excited to share his work amongst his peers. Having participated in similar festivals in the past, Evan is a true veteran when it comes to embodying the spirit of an arts festival! His painted works often show tranquil scenes, where his inked papers delve into fantastical realms! His works cater to a variety of audiences, so no matter your intrest, this incredible artist is sure to take your breath away!
